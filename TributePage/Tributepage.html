Dr. Abed Chowdhury

A Pioneer in Genetic Science, "From Village Roots to Global Innovations".

Dr. Abed Chowdhury stands infront of a paddy field.

Key info:
  • Early Life and Education: Born in 1956 in Moulvibazar, Bangladesh, Abed Chowdhury excelled academically, earning degrees from Dhaka University and the Oregon State Institute of Molecular Biology.
  • Groundbreaking Research: Discovered the "RecD" gene and pioneered apomixis, enabling seed production without fertilization.
  • Innovations in Agriculture: Developed high-yield rice varieties and colorful, nutrient-rich corn, enhancing food security and nutrition.
  • Literary Contributions: Authored numerous scientific articles, non-fiction books, and poetry in both Bengali and English.
